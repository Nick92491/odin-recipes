Stir Fry Recipe

Description

This stir fry recipe is all about the sauce a combination of umami and sugar set it off.

  • 1lb Steak like flank alternatively chicken
  • 2 tbsp
  • 1 orange
  • 1 Mexican Lager
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 small bunch fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar

    • Steps

    1. Mix together all ingredients in a bowl
    2. Add steak and let marinade for 2-24 hours
    3. Sear steak
