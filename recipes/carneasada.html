Carne Asada Recipe

Description

This carne asada recipe is just like dear old abuela used to make.

You'll need to sacrafice a cerveza to make it properly but trust me its worth it to get a tad less drunk for this cause.

  • 1lb Flank or skirt steak
  • 2 Limes
  • 1 orange
  • 1 Mexican Lager
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 small bunch fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp dark brown sugar

    • Steps

    1. Mix together all ingredients in a bowl
    2. Add steak and let marinade for 2-24 hours
    3. Sear steak
